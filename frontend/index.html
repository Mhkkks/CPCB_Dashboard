URSI-RCRS 2024 Research Visualization Platform

Research Background

This dashboard visualizes PM2.5 and Heat Index variability across Delhi and Jodhpur during El Niño periods.

The research focuses on: diurnal variability, thermal comfort, PM2.5 accumulation, and stable night-time conditions.

How To Download CPCB Data

  1. Open CPCB Dashboard
  2. Click on “Comparison Data”
  3. Select one station at a time
  4. Select: PM2.5, Temp, RH, AT
  5. Download hourly data in Excel (.xlsx) format
